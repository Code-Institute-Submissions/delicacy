    Recipe Name: Vegetarian nuddles

    Recipe Origin: Asia

    Pascatarian: Vegeterian

    Del Edit
    INGREDIENTS: Broccoli, carrots, soybeans, zucchini, soy sauce, olive oil, Nuddles, salt and pepper.
    REQUIRED TOOLS: Cutting board and knife, frying pan, carrot peeler, bowls.
    PREPARATION STEPS: 1. peel off the carrot skin and slice it into small pieces 2. Slice the broccoli and the zucchini 3. Boil a hot water and put some nuddles in a bowl and the hot water in it and close for few minutes 4. Fry the broccoli,carrots, zucchini and soybeans together in a frying pan with olive oil 5. Add some soy sauce to the already frying veges 6. Strain the nuddles in the hot water and add it to the frying veges and stair it very well until its well mixed up 7. Add some salt and pepper.
